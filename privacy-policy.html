Privacy Policy

Infoxera respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or interact with our products.

1. Who we are

Infoxera (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates this website and builds and operates digital products, including mobile and web applications (“Services”).

2. Information we collect

We aim to collect only what is necessary to run our website and provide our Services.

2.1 Information you provide

When you contact us (for example via email or a contact form), we may collect information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Message content and any information you choose to share

2.2 Automatically collected information

When you visit our website or use our Services, some technical information may be collected automatically by our hosting, analytics (if enabled), or infrastructure providers. This may include:

  • IP address
  • Device and browser type
  • Pages viewed and approximate usage timestamps
  • Referring/exit pages and basic performance logs

3. How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and communicate with you
  • To operate, maintain, and improve our website and Services
  • To monitor performance and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues
  • To comply with legal obligations where applicable

4. Cookies and tracking

We do not use cookies for advertising purposes on this website by default. If we add analytics or other measurement tools in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy accordingly.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited information with trusted service providers that help us operate the website and Services (such as hosting, email, security, or infrastructure providers). These providers are permitted to process information only to perform services on our behalf.

6. Data retention

We retain personal information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including responding to requests, maintaining records, and meeting legal or security requirements.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. International users

If you access our website or Services from outside your country, your information may be processed where our service providers operate. We take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards where required.

9. Your rights

Depending on your location and applicable law, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to certain processing of your personal information. To make a request, contact us using the details below.

10. Children’s privacy

Our website and Services are not intended for children under 13 (or the age required by local law). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above when changes are made.
